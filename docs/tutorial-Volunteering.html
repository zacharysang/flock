    Volunteering

    Volunteering your computing resources to flock is easy!

    When you volunteer your computer to help with distributed computing projects all you're doing is opening a browser tab! Your computer will, in the background, run code that helps other users do complex work, research, and solve difficult problems.

    Getting Started

    Visit the flock website, and click the button to volunteer. It's that easy! You can watch the page to see how your work is helping the cause!

    FAQ

    Is my computer at risk?

    No. Flock takes advantage of security built into your browser to keep our volunteers safe.

    Can I run flock in multiple tabs at once?

    Yes! You can run flock in as many tabs as you'd like, and for as long as you'd like.
